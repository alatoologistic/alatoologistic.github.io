Ala-Too Invest Logistic is a multifunctional company in Kyrgyzstan that provides a wide range of services to companies across different regions, including the EAEU countries, Southeast and Central Asia, the Middle East, China, and Europe.
In its activities, the company is also an integrated logistics operator: we serve the delivery of goods weighing from 1 kg by all types of land and air transport across Russia, to the countries of the Customs Union and the CIS, as well as goods deliveries from China to Europe and vice versa. At the request of the client, we can organize a complete chain of cargo delivery from anywhere in the world to Russia with consolidation in Kyrgyzstan, customs clearance, bring it to the warehouse, ensure storage, packaging and delivery to the final recipient.
Provision of surveyor services. We work with all types of goods and transportation. We inspect goods before, during and after loading. For example, for unloading a container with building stone, boxes are considered travertine, but in our case, the goods must be received and shipped, counting square meters.
Not without pride, we declare that we have fully mastered this niche and have earned a reputation as an accurate, responsible logistician and custodian inspector.
Today Ala-Too Invest Logistic is also a wholesale distributor of goods and services for the delivery and logistics of goods in the field of construction, household appliances, electronics and equipment for various sectors of the economy. In addition, we serve the delivery of goods that require special temperature conditions, such as cosmetics and pharmaceutical products. We have over 3000 sq.m. partner warehouses, more than 30 units of loading equipment and 30 employees in seaports around the world, including container terminals Khuaichun in Hong Kong, China and Jebel Ali in Dubai, UAE.

Main directions:

  • wholesale trade in construction goods, household appliances, electronics, textiles.
  • marketing research of the sales market of goods in the countries of the EAEU, Europe, China, the Middle East.
  • provision of services for the delivery of goods (logistics, merchandising).
  • consulting, customs, tax support services in foreign economic activity.

Our advantages:

  • we have partners in all regions of Russia, the countries of the Customs Union and beyond, so we can also act as an aggregator of their services.
  • trust and long-term relationships with contractors and staff is our credo.
  • our mission is to work professionally and honestly, receiving moral and material satisfaction.
